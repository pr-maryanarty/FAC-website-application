Why coding?

I’m a proponent of lifelong education, so within these years, I was trying so many different things. I am a former cheerleader, sport is my passion, now though I’m more interested in yoga and pilates - things that can also help our mind. I tried learning illustrations, design and project management, but nothing quite hit me as coding. Coding is both: instant gratification, namely: you see whether something worked out or not and at the same time contemplation and deep concentration, which takes a lot of brain power and drains you completely, but isn’t that how we know we’ve accomplished something? I strive for fulfillment and self-accomplishment, I believe that coding might help me do it. Web development is massive uncharted territory where you still are able to achieve great heights. I can’t say I’m particularly ambitious - to me it, perhaps, holds a more negative connotation - but I want to apply myself, I do believe I’m capable of overcoming new challenges. Changing career in a new country after you are 30 sounds like an interesting challenge, I’m willing to rise to.
